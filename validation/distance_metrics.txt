WHY APPROACH 1 DISTANCES ARE SHORTER THAN VERASET:
  1. Veraset covers all of Florida (long-distance trips included)
  2. Our POI dataset is Orange County metro (193K POIs)
  3. Nearest-POI search naturally finds local options first
  4. The PATTERN (shopping < work < recreation) still holds, see
     visualizations/KS_Validation_Distance.png (distance KS test PASSES,
     p=0.357, on the binned distance comparison)
