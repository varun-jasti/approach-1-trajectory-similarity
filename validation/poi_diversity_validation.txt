OVERALL COVERAGE
-----------------
Total POIs in dataset : 193,003
Unique POIs assigned  :  49,157  (25.5% of total dataset)
Activity types covered:      14 / 14  (100%)

WHAT 25.5% COVERAGE MEANS
---------------------------
This is healthy diversity. Using ALL 193K POIs would mean assigning
people to POIs that are geographically far or semantically wrong.
Nearest-POI + TAZ-filtering naturally restricts to locally relevant
options, which is the CORRECT behavior for realistic travel patterns.

FALLBACK ANALYSIS
------------------
County fallback used when no POI found in target activity TAZ.
This happens for rare activity types in low-density zones.
Fallback still provides a valid POI — just from a wider search area.
Mean MFC score for fallback rows: ~0.70  (still "Good" quality)
